Currencies in game

Primogems: The main currencies used to trade for Acquaint Fate for Intertwined Fate that are used to wish for characters banners (limited and standard banners)

Mora: the equivalent of gold in Teyvat. It is used for buying food, recipes, ascending weapons, characters and talents,.. It can be farmed through Leylines.

Resins (Original resins and fragile resins): Resins are use to claim rewards of farming domains and leylines

Original resins replenish 1 resin every 8 minutes and can be stored up to 160 resins (around a day worth). Can be spent on farming artifacts, talent books, weapon ascension materials, exp books, mora.

Fragile resins are items obtained as rewards from events or special quests and can be used to restore 160 original resins per use.

Condensed resin can be made with 40 original resin and 1 crystal core and can be used to claim farming rewards for double the rewards.
