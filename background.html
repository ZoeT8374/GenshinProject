Genshin Impact is an RPG, open-world exploration game developed by MIHOYO where players take the role of a traveler to the land in the world of Teyvat, follow through quests and solve puzzles to discover the secrets of the seven nations.
The game is action-based, which means characters can be put into a changeable team of 4 simultaneously. Different elements interact with each other to create powers and reactions in battles against environmental enemies. More details are found in the Combat section.

Quests

Quest is a major part of the game is its intricate main story lines and puzzles including quests where characteristics of each character are put into spotlights. Types of quests include:

  • Daily commissions: Small quests travelers take as commissions to earn rewards that refreshes daily. It can be region based with various types of commissions ranging from finding objects, fighting enemies to running errands for NPCs.
  • Archon quests: the major plotline of the game where major characters play different roles interacting with the traveler with cutscences animation and dialogues.
